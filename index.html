Conservation Matters

These magnificent cats face numerous threats including habitat loss, human conflict, climate change, and poaching. Many are rarely seen in the wild and are difficult to study. Supporting wildlife conservation organizations and protecting their natural habitats is crucial for their survival.

How You Can Help:

  • Reduce your carbon footprint to combat climate change
  • Raise awareness about these rare species
  • Support sustainable tourism that benefits local communities and wildlife
  • Never support the exotic pet trade
